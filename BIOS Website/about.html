Our Story

Dedicated to Your Success

BIOS Medical was founded in 2014. We have established a culture that puts our customers' needs first. We push ourselves every day to deliver quality products in a timely manner. Through this dedication, we built a new state of the art facility in 2020 in order to increase our capabilities to better serve our customers.

We are a Hernando, MS based company that believes in hard work and customer service. Our team of CNC programmers, operators, and front office admin personify the passion and integrity needed in this industry, driven by the constant improvement enabled by our leadership.

Our goal at BIOS is not to be just an ordinary Contract Manufacturer. We want to be a Support System to our customers. That is how we make a difference! At BIOS we truly believe in our ability to support you and welcome any opportunity to earn your trust. We look forward to your business!
